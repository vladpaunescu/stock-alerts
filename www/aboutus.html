Who we are



StockStalker is owned by TheFinancialBits Corp. A Romania based company with its main offices located in the city of Bucharest. The company is authorised and regulated by the Financial Services Authority (FSA) to offer Contracts For Difference (CFD). The company is a rapidly growing CFD provider in Europe and Asia and currently offers its portfolio of over 1000 instruments to over a million clients.



Your money is safe with us



When you open an account, TheFinancialBits Corp. will hold your money on a segregated basis, in accordance with the Financial Service Authority's client money rules.



Key milestones



StockStalker was founded in 2002. The Trading Platform enabled investors to trade on movements in the price of shares, indices, without having to buy or sell the underlying instrument.



2002 The original StockStalker company is founded, launching its PC-based online trading platform.

2005 StockStalker offer CFD on shares without commission.

2007 StockStalker launches a web based version, allowing Mac, Linux and smartphone users to trade online.

StockStalker introduces ETFs into its trading portfolio.

2009 StockStalker reaches more than 1,000,000 traders.

StockStalker has more than 2,000,000 monthly transactions.

StockStalker expands its international portfolio by offering over a 1000 different stocks from a wide range of global markets.

2012 StockStalker sets the goal of becoming the world's #1 CFD trading company.
